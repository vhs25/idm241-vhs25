Microinteractions - 06/03/20

Triggers:

The triggers for this microinteraction are scroll and tap/click.

Rules & Feedback:

On scroll, the content square sticks to the top of the screen, shrinking and becoming a dropdown bar that reopens and closes on click. Then if you scroll back up, it becomes a full-sized part of the page again.

Loops & Modes:

The change of conditions in this microinteraction happen on scroll. When you scroll down, the content square will become a sticky dropdown menu with a different trigger (tap). Then it will again become a part of the page when you scroll back up.
