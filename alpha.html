Before you click! - The animation of my chosen microinteraction only happens when the page is laded the first time. For me, once I've open the site in a browser and reload, it does not spin again.

Look in the top, left corner at the orange *star* !

https://css-tricks.com

Triggers:

Thee trigger for this microinteraction is on load. When the page is loaded the very first time, either when you click its link or type its url.

Rules:

After the page is loaded, the astrix/star shape in the header spins for around 2-3 seconds. That's all. It's very simple, but I was surprisingly delighted the first time I saw it. It added a sort of fun "welcome to thee page!"

Loops & Modes

The only thing that changes is the trigger conditions. This animation only shows on the first page load. Once a user has visited the page once, when they reload it or open it in another tab, it doesn't play for a while.
