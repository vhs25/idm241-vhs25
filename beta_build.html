Triggers:

The trigger to open and close this menu is a click/tap.

Rules & Feedback:

When the menu button is clicked, the menu options open up, and the colored background expands with it. When it is tapped again, it closes and shrinks back into a circle.

Loops & Modes:

The first tap triggers the menu to open, and the second tap triggers it to close. So the condition before the trigger determines whether the menu will open or close.
